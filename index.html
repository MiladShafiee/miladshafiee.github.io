Biography

My name is Milad shafiee. I am a robotics researcher focusing on the use of optimal control algorithms for control and planning of legged robots. Since 2017 I am a lead of dynamic and control group of SURENA 4, Iranian humanoid robot project, in the Center of Advanced Systems and Technologies(CAST) at University of Tehran, where I have worked with the humanoid robot SURENA 3 and Mini-SURENA.

Interests

  • Autonomous and Robust Motion Planning
  • Model Predictive Control
  • Optimization-Based Control
  • Reinforcement Learning
